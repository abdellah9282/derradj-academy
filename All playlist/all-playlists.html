    About Derradj Academy

    Derradj Academy is a leading Algerian e-learning platform founded by Abdellah Derradj in collaboration with experienced university professors. It is dedicated to empowering Algerian university students in scientific and technical fields through accessible, high-quality education tailored to their academic needs.

    • 🎓 Courses tailored to Algerian university curricula
    • 📚 Downloadable PDFs for lectures, TDs, and exams
    • 🧑‍🏫 Weekly live sessions with expert instructors
    • 💬 Direct communication via chat and video
    • 💳 Easy payment via BaridiMob and CCP
    • 🌍 Equal access for all Algerian students
